my work

Hi. It's Aliena.

I’m an Artist from Switzerland.

My Dream is to open my own Cafe and draw Manga for a living.

I absolutely love coffee and music.

I spend most of my time painting and drawing, watching Anime or K-Drama and listening to music.

When I’m not doing any of those things I’m probably playing Minecraft or hanging out with my friends.

I’m an Aquarius and INFP-T.

digital

Die digitale Kunst hat mir in letzter Zeit immer mehr zugesprochen. Von überall aus kann ich auf meinem Ipad gestalten und meiner Kreativität freien Lauf lassen.
